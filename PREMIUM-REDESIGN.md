## 🎯 Design-Prinzipien

### **Typography:**
- Headlines: 3.5-5.5rem (BOLD, 800-900 weight)
- Body: 1-1.375rem
- Negative Letter-Spacing (-0.03em/-0.04em)
- Inter Font (wie Apple)

### **Colors:**
- Primary: **Black** (#000000)
- Secondary: **White** (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: **Blue** (#0066FF)
- Grays: 50-900 (Tailwind-style)

### **Spacing:**
- Sections: 10rem padding (massive)
- Gaps: 3-5rem (großzügig)
- Whitespace: Überall!

### **Images:**
- Full-bleed (100% width/height)
- High-quality Stock Photos
- Dark Overlays (60-80%)
- Object-fit: cover
